Quiz:

1. Name 3 User Interface Strategies.
2. Is UX Greater than UI design?
3. Is Qualitative research about looking at the raw data?
4. What went wrong with MetroUI and Microsoft


Answers:
1. Double Coding; Data Grouping; Symmetry; Data Density; Contrast; Consistency
2. Yes, UX included UI design and much more
3. No qualitative research is about answering the why, or to find out abstract information not raw data or statistics. 
4. The broke the consistency of their main product and people panicked.
